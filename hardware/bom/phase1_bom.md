## Notes

- Enclosure cost assumes Xometry SLA printing. Self-printing drops cost to ~$3–6 for resin per plug.
- No separate lid needed — the endcap is integral to the plug body.
- Men's batteries (402025, 300mAh) provide ~20+ hours of active practice.
- Women's batteries (401730, ~150mAh) provide ~8–12 hours of active practice due to narrower shaft.
- Batteries MUST have JST-PH 2.0mm connectors to match XIAO battery pads.
- If batteries arrive with bare leads, use the JST connector kit to add plugs.
- Order 1–2 extra batteries per size as spares (they're cheap and fragile).
- O-rings: need 2 per plug (6 for men's, 4 for women's = 10 total). Packs include spares.
- XIAO boards come with USB-C cables, but extras are useful for simultaneous charging.
