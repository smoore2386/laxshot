## Notes

- Enclosure cost assumes Xometry SLA printing. Self-printing drops cost to ~$5–10 for resin.
- Batteries MUST have JST-PH 2.0mm connectors to match XIAO battery pads.
- If batteries arrive with bare leads, use the JST connector kit to add plugs.
- Order 1–2 extra batteries as spares (they're cheap and fragile).
- XIAO boards come with USB-C cables, but extras are useful for simultaneous charging.
